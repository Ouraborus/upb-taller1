Fibonacci Layout

A simple layout inspired by Fibonacci Sequence, from left to right, each section decrease his size. Creating a layout with seven areas. The Fibonacci sequence is named after Italian mathematician Leonardo of Pisa, known as Fibonacci. His 1202 book "Liber Abaci" introduced the sequence to Western European mathematics.
